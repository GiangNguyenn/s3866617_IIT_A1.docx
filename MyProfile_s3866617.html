    Project Idea: Social Platform for Coffee Lovers


    Thoughout the history of humanity, coffee is one of the most well-known essential that people enjoy on daily basis. Its community comprises of a variety types of people, regardlesss of age, background, genders, ethnicity, etc. It is so crucial that it has been merging into our day without us being concious about having it, a coffee in the morning is a more like a cuklture than a personal routine. Therefore, the project I thought of is a platform that could connect coffee lovers in the world, where they can share their thoughts on their favourite cup of coffee, e.g rate it, or criticise a type of coffee that they do not enjoy. Moreover, this will be in the form of a social-meida-like application, so its main goal is a user-friendly, state-of-the-art sort of application, which would fit it into the current activities of the modern society really quickly.

    According to the currernt statistics, the percentage of American population who drink at least a cup of coffee per day is 64% (foodandwine.com ). The statistics fluctuates from country to country yet it is always high. And the compound growth of the Coffee retail Market is 4.1% ( Global Coffee Shops Market Analysis, Trends, and Forecasts 2019-2025 - ResearchAndMarkets.com), which can be considered quite high for a traditional industry, and the projected developmental speed does not seems to slow down in the future. Therefore, with the fact that there has a not been a popular social media application designed only for the coffeeholics, the is a potential share in the market for the application idea. Definitely, if the app was launched and maintain properly, it will profit

    Fundamentally, the bare bones of the platform will inherit from many other modern social media platforms and some other unique features will be developed alongside. Moreover, the process of development would put its focus on the user interface to set up a clean, minimal yet appealing display for the app. This is because most of the people who use social media platforms are later generation, obviously, the younger people are, the more of visual thinkers they are. An integrated, beautiful UI would be critical for an app to make it way to success in the society. Additionally, a reliable database for the app should also be constructed over time as the app should be able to provide users with sufficient information to maintain its impacts.

    Initially, the idea of this niche social media platform came from a similar application called Untappd. It was created for craft beer lovers in all over the World. Despite the fact the platform aims for a very niche community, its revenue is up to $10m per anum ( Owler.com) in just 5 years since the day it was released. Based on that single example of social media for people of the same interest, we could possibly assume that another platform for an even bigger community would be profitable.

    In terms of technical aspect, the most crucial functionalities of the app would take after the features of the current trendy social media application, namely:
    • Ease of Connectivity
    • Ability to Extensively Customize Individual Profiles
    • Ability to Expand User Following
    • Easy Integration With Other Social Media Platforms.
    • Privacy and Security.
    • Notification and News Feed

      • Source: Youngupstarts.com

    The general workflow of the app would be: (Expectations)

    1. Open the app.
    2. The users can look up for the coffee location that they are currently at.
    3. Check in at the coffee shop with the specific type of coffee that the user is drinking.
    4. The users can take and attach a picture of the coffee
    5. The users are also permitted to rate the coffee and describe its unique flavours with available tags or by their own captions.
    6. Users can also tag a friend/partner who also have an account.
    7. The users can post their checkin at the location after customising it.
    8. The users would see the posts on their social feeds
    9. The users would also be able to see other's posts of whom they follow or connected

    On User's Personal Profile, the app would display features and properties related to the number of coffee cups that user has had over a period of time. Moreover, it would as well calculate the average rating of the users, the types of coffee that they have been trying so far( e.g Flat White, Cold Brew, etc.), their networking with others in the app, and the list goes on.


    As time goes by, the app would be able to garther rating statistics from users and be able to rank the coffee shops, specialty coffee, etc.; from all over the world And recommend other users about the popular locations, drinks, information regarding that drink, previous checkin's reviews/comments while they are travelling to the area. Else, they could use the search engine to locate the good coffee shops that they want to go to in the future.

    During the initial stage of the development, a minimal team should be enough for building the product. Since Python is not only a powerful yet user-friendly language, I have considered using it as the baseline for the product. There are some other useful external tools, libraries, frameworks that go with Python as well. Django or Flask are considered the most appropriate for the project since I want to commence with a web application. Later on, other languages and frameworks will be utilised for setting the app on other Operating Systems.

    In order to build the application, the technical work may not be very complicated at the beginning, thereby, basic knowledge of Web App development (both back-end and front-end), Testing, Data scienceand business would be sufficient. However, by the time the application has more users, it may get real complex and require thorough research and a certain amount of work put into it. The farther the product progresses, third-party services should be used for collaboration in a team, such services as Slack, Atlassian. etc. will be neccesary.

    In the current context of modern society, provided the application would be developed properly and the progression advancces as expected. It is predictable that the application would be commercially monetary in a foreseeable future. One of the biggest factors that may contribute to yeilding a good outcome is the essential demand of sharing and connecting between individuals in modern society.Especially in an enormous community of coffee lovers, the app provide the service that can handle that need. And also, since the projection of rthe retail industry is very positive, collaboration between this product and those major retail brands will possibly reusult in financial mutual benefits for both parties, more generally, both industries.
